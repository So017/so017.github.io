My Story

Hi, I'm Sonia! I'm a data analyst.
I've always been curious about different kinds of things—philosophy, coding, and even how to make the best milk foam for coffee. It is satisfying to learn new knowledge.

Before my journey into data analytics, I worked in supply chain. Initially, data analysis wasn't expected in my role. One day, I became curious about how shipment performance was impacting sales, what the bestsellers were, etc. I started to organize the data, find insights, and was happy that my teammates found my presentation helpful. I gradually discovered my intuition and passion for data analytics.

Due to a change in my life situation, I moved to a new place and took this opportunity to try something I had never done before—running my own coffee shop. It has been such a valuable experience, not just in handling everything on my own, but also in building relationships with customers.

At the same time, I kept building my data analytics skills through self-study and projects. I decided that it is a skill that I not only want to maintain but also want to keep mastering and contributing to.

Other fun facts about me: I am interested in psychology and understanding different cultures. I am fascinated by people—how they think and why they do what they do. I have been learning Thai and Japanese. I also enjoy photography and mindfulness. Both allow me to slow down, gain clarity, and appreciate the little things in life that often go unnoticed.
